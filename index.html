What is music?

Music is something that is known to everyone on the planet and has been around for thousands of years with a deep connection to cultures and ways of life.

The range of music is something that can never be understated and has the ability to capture the attention and awe of anyone. Music is one of the most diverse form of media there is as there are potentially infinite combinations of instruments, vocals, genres and meaning that goes into each and every piece of music.

Among music there are musical artists who have taken music and elevated it by putting their own beliefs, messages and their very being into it creating something that is entirely unique to them and their listeners to enjoy.
